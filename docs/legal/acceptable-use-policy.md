# Mad Thinker Acceptable Use Policy

Version dated 2026-04-21

This Acceptable Use Policy ("Policy") governs use of the Mad Thinker platform and the EmeraldWatersAnglers mobile application (together, the "Service") provided by Mad Thinker, Inc. ("Mad Thinker," "we," "our," or "us"). This Policy applies to all users of the Service, including anglers, guides, researchers, public users, and anyone accessing the Service through a lodge, conservation agency, or directly. By using the Service, you agree to this Policy. We may modify this Policy at any time by posting an updated version; your continued use of the Service after any change constitutes acceptance.

## No Tolerance for Objectionable Content or Abusive Users

Mad Thinker has zero tolerance for objectionable content and abusive users. We will act on reports of violations and may remove content or terminate accounts without prior notice.

## Prohibited Uses

You may not use the Service, and you may not authorize, permit, or encourage any other person to use the Service:

- In any way that violates applicable law, regulation, governmental order, or decree;
- To violate the legal rights of any person or entity, including intellectual property, publicity, or privacy rights;
- To post, upload, transmit, or share content that is unlawful, defamatory, libelous, obscene, pornographic, sexually explicit, violent, hateful, discriminatory, or that incites violence against any person or group;
- To harass, threaten, bully, stalk, impersonate, or abuse any other user, or to engage in conduct that a reasonable person would find offensive;
- To post content that depicts or promotes cruelty to fish or wildlife beyond lawful catch-and-release or harvest practices, poaching, illegal take, or any violation of fish, wildlife, or conservation laws;
- To post photos of identifiable individuals without their consent, or to post personally identifying information about any other person without authorization;
- To submit false, misleading, or fabricated catch reports, locations, species identifications, or conservation data intended to deceive other users, lodges, conservation agencies, or researchers;
- To attempt to gain unauthorized access to the Service, other users' accounts, or any connected service, device, data, account, or network;
- To distribute spam, malware, or any software or code intended to disrupt, damage, or gain unauthorized access to any system;
- To interfere with, disrupt, or impair the Service or any other user's ability to use the Service;
- To use the Service in any application or situation where failure of the Service could lead to death or serious bodily injury of any person, or to severe physical or environmental damage;
- To use the Service in any way that encourages, promotes, or facilitates illegal, harmful, or fraudulent activities.

## Reporting Violations

If you become aware of any content or user conduct that violates this Policy, please report it immediately by emailing **support@madthinkertech.com**. Lodge administrators may also report violations by contacting us through their account representative.

We commit to reviewing all reports within 24 hours of receipt.

## Our Response to Violations

We reserve the right, but do not assume the obligation, to investigate any suspected violation of this Policy. Upon determining, in our sole discretion, that a violation has occurred, we may, with or without prior notice:

- Remove or hide any content that violates this Policy;
- Suspend or permanently terminate any account, including revoking access for users who received the Service through a lodge, conservation agency, or subscription;
- Ban violating users from the Service;
- Disclose or report activity to lodges, conservation agencies, employers, law enforcement, regulators, or other appropriate third parties.

We may take any or all of these actions at our discretion, including for conduct we reasonably believe is harmful, even if a specific provision of this Policy is not explicitly cited.

## Cooperation

If you become aware of any violation of this Policy, you will promptly notify us and provide reasonable assistance, as requested, to stop or remedy the violation.
